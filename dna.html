Nucleotides, are the essential components of DNA and RNA, and are not inherently complex.
In the context of DNA sequences and genetics, nucleotides are the building blocks that make
up the genetic information in living organisms. The four nucleotides present in DNA are :

Adenine (A), Cytosine (C), Guanine (G), and Thymine (T).
